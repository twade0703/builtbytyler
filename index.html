The approach

Every product starts as a sketch and ends as something real.

I'm Tyler — I design and build mechanical and software products end to end. The process, the iterations, the dead ends and everything they teach are the whole point. I work through the hard parts so you don't have to — turning builds most people think are too difficult into something you can learn to make yourself.
