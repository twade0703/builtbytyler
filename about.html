About me

Built by hand, from the first sketch to the finished product.

BuiltByTyler is the studio of Tyler Wade — a 23-year-old designer and engineer who designs, prototypes and builds original mechanical and software products end to end.

The craft

From aerodynamics and materials to propulsion and structure, every product is grounded in real mechanical design — refined through iteration until it's right.

Mechanical & software

Beyond the hardware, I build the tools around it — custom GUIs, utilities and CAD packages that make engineering workflows faster and cleaner.

The process is the point

The iterations, the fallbacks, the learning — that's what drives every build. In my free time I'm hiking, skating, biking, or making whatever my mind produces next.
